📖 About ASL

American Sign Language (ASL) is a natural language that serves as the predominant sign language of Deaf communities in the United States and English-speaking parts of Canada. We provide comprehensive learning materials covering 250+ commonly used signs.

💡 Learning Tips

  • Start Simple: Begin with basic greetings and common words
  • Watch Carefully: Pay attention to hand shapes, positions, and movements
  • Mirror Movements: Practice by facing a mirror to check your positioning
  • Consistent Practice: Regular practice helps build muscle memory
  • Use Both Methods: Combine videos and images for comprehensive learning
